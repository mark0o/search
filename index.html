Games

Eurotruck Sim

Euro Truck Simulator 2 is a realistic vehicle simulation game where players drive trucks across a detailed map of Europe, completing cargo deliveries while managing and expanding their own trucking company.

Escapists 2

The Escapists 2 is a strategy game where players take on the role of inmates trying to escape from various prisons by following routines, crafting items, and using stealth or combat to achieve their goals.

Prison Architect

Prison Architect is a simulation game where players design and manage a private prison, balancing inmate needs with operational demands while preventing riots and escapes.

Raft

Raft is a multiplayer oceanic survival game where players start on a small raft and must gather debris from the sea to survive, expand their floating home, and fend off dangers like man-eating sharks.
